Privacy Policy – Respieck

Last Updated: January 2025

Respieck – Responsive Tester is designed with privacy in mind. This extension does not collect, store, transmit, or share any kind of personal or usage data.

Information We Do NOT Collect

Respieck does NOT collect any of the following:

  • Personal information (name, email, address, etc.)
  • Browsing history
  • User activity, clicks, scroll data, keystrokes
  • Analytics, tracking data, or identifiers
  • Device information or IP address
  • Files, cookies, or site data

Permissions Explanation

The extension uses activeTab, scripting, tabs, and access to all URLs only to allow you to preview and test a webpage in different device sizes. No data is collected, sent, or stored.

Third-Party Sharing

Respieck does not share data with any third party.

Changes to This Policy

If the privacy policy is updated, the new version will be posted on this page.
